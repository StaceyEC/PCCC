Lacrosse Party Supplies
You've won the LAX championship, now it's time to celebrate with lacrosse party supplies! You can set up your party with matching cups, paper plates, and napkins that feature the headline 'Lacrosse LAX to the Max' Your child will be running around in excitement for decorations featuring a green and dark gray theme with lacrosse stick prints.

Invite the whole lacrosse team over to the party and start decorating! First, shoot swirl decorations and honeycomb decorations in the ceiling to feel that excitement in the air! Next, set the table. Use a green cascade centerpiece to add some sparkle to the table. Make place settings with lacrosse plates and lacrosse napkins. Don't forget to complete your place settings with solid-color plastic forks, knives, and spoons! With disposable tableware, you'll be able to rally the team and clean up this lacrosse party in one play!

All winners deserve a prize! The kids will be poke checking each other to grab these party favors. Fill lacrosse favor cups or drawstring backpacks with lacrosse party favors. Your little LAX players will love temporary tattoos, wristbands, lanyards, and decals. Get them huddled and dressed up for team photos around the lacrosse balloon bouquet!

Browse lacrosse party supplies and other party decorations to inspire your lacrosse party theme!
